Waffles

Description

A simple but delicious recipe for morning breakfast waffles. Includes an ingredient list, as well as how to put things together

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
